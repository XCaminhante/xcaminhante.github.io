What is this project about

Vesta Configuration Management System is an advanced software configuration management system. It handles source revision control and automated construction. It is the result of over 10 years of research and development at the Compaq/Digital Systems Research Center.

Vesta CMS is GNU-licensed software. This is an atempt to backup and revive the software.
