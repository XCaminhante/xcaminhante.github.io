Graphical Smileys

To insert a smiley, just type in your favourite from the table below. They're only converted to an icon though if they are surrounded by whitespace.

Markup Display Markup Display Markup Display Markup Display
X-( :D <:( :o
:( :) B) :))
;) /!\ <!> (!)
:-? :\ >:> |)
:-( :-) B-) :-))
;-) |-) (./) {OK}
{X} {i} {1} {2}
{3} {*} {o}    

Also, you can add any image of your theme img folder by the Icon macro.
